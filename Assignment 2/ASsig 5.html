My Favorite Hobby

Why I Like It

Playing chess is my favorite hobby because it challenges my mind and improves my concentration. I enjoy thinking carefully about each move and planning strategies to outsmart my opponent. Chess helps me develop patience, problem-solving skills, and logical thinking. Every game feels different and exciting, which keeps me interested and motivated to play more.

How I Spend Time Doing It

I usually spend my free time playing chess with my friends or online against players from around the world. Sometimes I practice by solving puzzles or learning new openings and tactics to improve my skills. I try to play at least one or two games every day, especially in the evening. This routine helps me relax while also sharpening my brain.

My routine

I usually play chess in the evening after finishing my daily work or studies. I set up the board or open a chess app on my phone and play a few games with friends or online opponents. Sometimes I practice tactics or learn new strategies to improve my skills. Spending this quiet time with chess helps me relax and keeps my mind sharp every day.
